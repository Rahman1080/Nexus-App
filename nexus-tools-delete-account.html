Delete Account & Data – Nexus Tools

Users of Nexus Tools may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email us at support@yourdomain.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account
  3. Send the request from the email associated with your Nexus Tools account

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information (email & authentication data)
  • Saved tools, preferences, and settings
  • Any synced or stored app data associated with the account

Data that may be retained

Minimal data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. This data is retained for no longer than 30 days and is then permanently deleted.

Deletion timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7–14 business days. A confirmation email will be sent once the deletion is complete.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
